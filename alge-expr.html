What expressions?

Expressions are statements of value that can change. This means that it is not something that you can evaluate, which is why there are no equal signs. THe value of an expression can change in algebra depending on the value of the variables in the equation. A way for you to understand this is with currency. In one United States Dollar, there is four quarters. If you have 2 dollars, then you have 8 quarters worth of money.
