Introduction to Area

Area is the amount of space taken up by a 2 dimensional object. This space is measured in square unites, but you can use other types of units when applying area to real life situations. These can include metric and imperial units. Because area is space taken up by two dimensions, the units chosen is squared

The rectangle is 3 units wide, and 6 units long. That means there are 18 units inside the rectangle across two axes, which means that this area of this rectangle is 18 units squared.
