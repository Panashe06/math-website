Volume of Triangular Prisms

You may be familiar with the formula for the area of a triangle. A = (1/2)bh . To calculate the volume of a triangular prism, it is the same formula multiplied by the length (longest side) of the prism, like so: A = L (1/2)BH

Think of the volume of a triangular prism as how much space is taken up when “ L “ amount of triangles are stacked on top of each other.
